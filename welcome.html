About MyCash


College can be rough. The last thing you need during these crucial years is financial anxiety. We know it is hard to keep track of your spendings and harder to spend less when you don't know where your cash is going. We don't want you to worry about budget constraints, and that's why we exist.


HOW IT WORKS

Control your expenditures through simple steps


INPUT

Every time you spend money, enter the amount you have spent and choose the category that expenditure belongs to.

TRACKING

View a detailed history of your spendings and choose how you want it to be sorted.

STATS

Visualize statistics about your expenditures per category and time span through charts.
